Registration for the ISCHE 45 conference in Natal, Brazil, will open in April 2024 (in Conftool).
The early registration rate ends on May 20, 2024.

ISCHE Membership

Please note that ISCHE has moved to a membership structure whereby individuals can join ISCHE via the ISCHE website. Member benefits include discounted registration fees at annual conferences and discounted subscriptions to the journal Paedagogica Historica

Membership of ISCHE is required prior to registration for ISCHE 45 (the conference in 2024), in order to be eligible for any associated discount. Please sign up at http://www.ische.org/join-ische.

Additional Information

Participants can also register on the online system for optional ISCHE events, such as the Welcome Reception, Dinner for Early Career Researchers (paid - value will be announced soon).

The ISCHE 45 programme will include the names and email addresses of all registered participants unless you request that your name and email not be included. If you wish to opt out of having your name and email included in the program, please contact ische45@ische.org .

Refund Policy

100% refund (minus a bank processing fee) will be granted to cancellations received prior to May 20, 2024. 

Any cancellations between May 21 and June 21, 2024 will be refunded at 50%. No refunds will be available for cancellations received after June 21, 2024.

To request a refund please contact ische45@ische.org and include the registration confirmation number that you received by email.

Please allow 7-10 business days for a refund to be processed.


* Please choose your registration rate carefully and in accordance with your selected mode of presentation and membership status. Refunds for wrong registrations will be applicable only until June 21, 2024.
